Pawfect Match

Pawfect Match is an app designed to address the crisis of stray dogs in Miami. It connects rescue dogs with potential adopters and foster parents, ensuring they find safe and loving homes quickly. By reaching a wider audience, it expands the chances for dogs to be adopted or fostered. The app incorporates a matching algorithm to prevent mismatched placements, considering factors such as lifestyle and dog temperament. It also facilitates seamless communication and support among rescue organizations, adopters, and foster parents, faciliting and secure the adoption process. With Pawfect Match, we can make a significant impact, saving more lives and providing more foster homes in Miami. In the development of Pawfect Match, I utilized React, to create an intuitive and responsive user interface. Additionally, I designed and implemented a database using MySQL to store and manage the app's data effectively. To power the server-side functionalities, I utilized Node.js, enabling efficient communication between the app's frontend and backend.

UKG

We were incredibly lucky to have UKG, join a cohort's event at my school Brainstation. UKG is a software company and a leading provider of HR, payroll and workforce management solutions.This project was a collaborative effort with my UX/design teammate at Brainstation. We brainstormed and proposed an innovative idea aimed at assisting individuals who are looking to change their careers or grow within their current company. Our goal was to create a dedicated platform that would cater to their specific needs and provide a seamless experience. By developing this platform, we recognized the opportunity to not only benefit individuals but also contribute to the growth and market development of UKG in the career searching space. The platform would position UKG as a leader in supporting employees' career aspirations and attracting new talent to companies associated with UKG. Through our collaboration, we focused on creating a user-centric design that prioritized usability and a visually appealing interface. Our goal was to ensure that users could easily navigate the platform, input their skills and preferences, and receive tailored career recommendations.
